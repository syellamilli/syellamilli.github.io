About Me

The TL;DR

My name is Shivaram, but I often go by Shiv! I am driven to explore, grow, learn, and make the world a better place. These ideals guide me in everything I do, from my hobbies to my career. I am currently searching for either a part time data scientist role or a summer 2021 data science internship. If you have a position that you think I would be a good fit for, please reach out below!

Career

I am a data scientist who is focused on bringing about meaningful change. My strong analytical mindset enables me to tackle the most complex problems in a systematic and efficient manner. Additionally, my expertise in mathematics and Python means that I am capable of jumping into any area of machine learning, from signal processing to NLP, and quickly get myself to the cutting edge. I am currently looking for part time data science opportunities and summer 2021 data science internships. I am confident that I can provide immense value to any team that I join; my references (available upon request) can testify to this. If you think I would be a good fit for any positions that you are aware of, I would love to hear from you!

Hobbies

Our universe is a beautiful place that constantly leaves me awestruck; I want to see and experience as much of it as I can. To explore the sea, I SCUBA dive; to explore the land, I hike; to explore the sky, I stargaze. Where I can, I try to capture feelings evoked by these spaces through photography.

My other hobbies revolve around challenging myself mentally and physically. I love playing soccer, but COVID-19 has made this infeasible. While in quarantine, I have shifted that competitive energy to chess. As with all things, I still have much room for growth, but I am definitely improving!
